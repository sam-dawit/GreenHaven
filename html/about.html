Welcome to Angel Comfort Care

At Angel Comfort Care, we are dedicated to providing exceptional home care services with a personal touch. Our history is rich with stories of compassion, and our mission is to make a positive difference in the lives of those we serve.

Our Mission

  • To provide excellent care in a home-like environment
  • To meet and exceed all requirements of adult family homes as stipulated by the State of Washington and the needs of our residents
  • To provide excellent customer relations; we give our residents and families the courtesy and respect we would like to have ourselves.
  • To fostering a much more intimate environment for both residents and staff.

Our mission is to empower individuals to live with dignity, comfort, and independence in their own homes. We value respect, integrity, and compassion in every interaction.
